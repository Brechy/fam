Cleaning

All moms need help keeping the nest clean. Connect with like minded moms who can show up when you need that extra help. Fam members can help keep things tidy for new moms and experienced moms alike. Make it a group Fam event or just have one Fam member visit you every once in a while to help tidy up. From light vacuuming to keeping the sink clear of dishes, every mom could use a Fam to help keep up on cleaning.

Cooking

Working moms and new moms alike share the same challenge, keeping the refrigerator stocked. Fam members can connect to help do weekly meal preps together, bring an extra lasanga over to a new mom, or just have a nice lunch together. Your Fam can be there for you during those crazy times when you need an extra hand to keep everyone fed.

Hanging Out

Hanging out isn't just for the boys. Moms need quality time too, so be sure to get it by connecting with other like minded moms. Plan park dates, no-kid brunches, or just invite your Fam over to chat and binge watch Netflix! The important thing, is that you connect with other moms so that you are not isolated. Being a mom can get tough, whether you are a working mom or a stay at home mom, so keep in touch with women who understand you.
